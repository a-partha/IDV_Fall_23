Manchester United Football Club

Transfer-Ins | Summer Window | Deadline Day

Man Utd sign Sofyan Amrabat on a season-long loan from Fiorentina. The midfielder was one of the star performers for Morocco at the 2022 Fifa Men's World Cup.

Since Shaw and Malacia have picked up long-term injuries, they also sign fullback Sergio Reguilon from Spurs on loan for the season.

Goalkeeper Altay Bayindir joins on a permanent deal from Fenerbahce as a backup.
